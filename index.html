About

I’m a third year graduate student in the department of mathematics at UC Berkeley. I am usually curious about anything with the word 'algebraic' in front of it. My research interests include

  • Population oncology, precision health solutions for non-communicable disease care in low-socioeconomic settings
  • Data interoperability, data diversity, data acquisition, and representation in data
